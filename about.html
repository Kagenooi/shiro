Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide expert advice and management services to help our clients achieve their financial goals and build wealth over time.

Our vision is to become a market leader, by developing innovative investment strategies, expanding into new markets, and attracting top talent to the company.

Our aim is

Through investments, promote the development and strengthening of entrepreneurship in Armenia, becoming a reliable and trustworthy partner for entrepreneurs, promoting exports, creating new workplaces, balanced development of the regions of the Republic of Armenia, identifying and financing of ambitious projects with a great potential for development and growth, as a result providing profit to the fund’s participants.

Investments are made in the following fields:

  • Industry
  • Agriculture
  • Information Technology
  • Construction
  • Provision of services
  • Trade
  • Tourism and hotel business
  • Healthcare and pharmacy
  • Energy and other infrastructures
  • * Organization of new production and expansion of production;
  • * Import substitution, organization of production with export potential;
  • * Organization of production with the use of new technologies or innovation-driven production
  • * Creation of new or additional workplaces, the preservation of existing workplaces
